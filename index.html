Unbox and Connect: Open the Trezor box and connect the device to your computer using the provided USB cable. Visit Trezor Start Page: Go to trezor.io/start in your browser. Download Trezor Suite: Download and install Trezor Suite, the application used for managing your Trezor device. Initialize Device: Open Trezor Suite and follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your device. This will involve setting up a PIN and generating a recovery seed. Secure Your Seed: Write down the recovery seed on the provided card and store it securely. This is crucial for recovering your wallet if needed. Complete Setup: Follow the prompts to finish the setup and start using your Trezor for secure cryptocurrency transactions.

Getting Started with Your Trezor Device

Welcome to Trezor.io/Start®. Here you will find all the information you need to set up and use your Trezor device. Follow these steps to ensure your device is secure and ready to use:

  1. Connect your Trezor device to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  2. Open the Trezor Suite application on your computer.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your device, including creating a new wallet and setting up a PIN.
  4. Ensure you write down your recovery seed and store it in a safe place.
  5. Once setup is complete, you can start managing your cryptocurrencies securely.

If you encounter any issues while setting up your Trezor device, visit our support page for troubleshooting tips and resources. You can also contact our support team for further assistance.
